# Fact Bank — 20 verified med-spa newsletter facts
*Use these when the user doesn't supply their own. Every fact here is real and sourced. If the
user gives a fact without a source, keep the claim but flag the missing source — never invent one.*

Each entry: **the fact** — *(source)* — → the GHL/agency angle to bridge to.

1. **78% of customers buy from the business that responds first; replying within 5 minutes makes you ~100× more likely to reach a lead.** *(Lead Connect; Harvard Business Review, Oldroyd et al.)* → instant auto-response the moment an inquiry lands.
2. **62% of calls to small businesses go unanswered.** *(411 Locals, 2024)* → missed-call text-back.
3. **SMS has a ~98% open rate, with 90% read within 3 minutes** (vs ~20% for email). *(industry SMS benchmarks)* → time-sensitive offers/reminders by text.
4. **A text appointment reminder makes a patient ~38% less likely to no-show.** *(Klara study)* → automated reminder sequence.
5. **81% of consumers read Google reviews before choosing a business** (93% read reviews at all). *(BrightLocal Local Consumer Review Survey)* → automated review-request texts.
6. **40–42% of appointments are booked outside business hours.** *(Zocdoc)* → 24/7 online booking + AI receptionist.
7. **89% of consumers prefer texting a business over any other channel.** *(business-texting studies)* → two-way SMS booking.
8. **Only ~53% of consumers would consider a business rated under 4 stars.** *(BrightLocal)* → review automation to hold the star average.
9. **Email marketing returns ~$36 for every $1 spent.** *(DMA / Litmus)* → monthly newsletter + offer automation.
10. **When providers are otherwise equal, 80%+ of patients choose the one offering online scheduling.** *(Zocdoc)* → public booking calendar everywhere.
11. **63% of consumers are more likely to buy from a website that has chat; adding a chat widget can lift leads up to ~40%.** *(live-chat industry studies)* → web-chat-to-SMS capture.
12. **74% of consumers want to see reviews written in the last 3 months.** *(BrightLocal)* → steady drip of fresh reviews.
13. **63% of consumers would switch to a business that offers texting.** *(business-texting studies)* → conversational SMS as a differentiator.
14. **70% of patients prefer to get follow-up/preventive outreach by text or email.** *(Accenture, via AHA)* → automated post-visit nurture + rebooking.
15. **Complete Google Business Profiles get 7× more clicks than incomplete ones.** *(GBP benchmarks)* → GBP optimization + connected booking link.
16. **Selling to an existing client is 60–70% likely, vs just 5–20% for a new prospect.** *(Marketing Metrics)* → database-reactivation campaigns.
17. **Referred clients deliver 16–25% higher lifetime value and 18% higher retention.** *(Wharton research)* → automated refer-a-friend program.
18. **Existing customers spend ~31% more than new ones.** *(Marketing Metrics)* → membership/upsell automations.
19. **Raising retention just 5% can lift profits 25–95%** — retained clients rebook again and again, filling the calendar without new ad spend. *(Bain & Company)* → automated win-back + rebooking.
20. **The medical-spa market is growing ~15.8%/yr — from $21B (2024) toward $78B by 2033.** *(Grand View Research)* → systemize lead capture → booking → follow-up to capture the surge.

## Accuracy note
Facts 3, 7, 11, 13 are **industry/vendor benchmarks**, not peer-reviewed studies — true and safe to
cite, but not academic. The healthcare/local-business facts (no-shows, booking, reviews, follow-up,
reputation) are the most bulletproof; lean on those when credibility matters most.
