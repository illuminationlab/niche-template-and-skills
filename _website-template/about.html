Our Story

We built [PRODUCT_NAME] because
med spas deserved better.

Every CRM on the market was built for somebody else. Salesforce was built for enterprise sales teams. HubSpot was built for digital marketers. Mindbody was built for yoga studios. Even the tools that claimed to serve med spas were either salon software with a medical add-on or hospital EMR systems stripped down for aesthetics. None of them understood the actual business of running a med spa. We did. And it frustrated us.

The Problem We Kept Seeing

Brilliant operators. Broken tools.

We watched med spa owners — brilliant clinicians and sharp business operators — spend their nights answering voicemails, their weekends building spreadsheets, and their mornings logging into four different platforms just to figure out if their marketing was working. We saw front desk teams drowning in a constant juggle of calls, DMs, texts, and walk-ins — with no single system to manage it all. We saw practices spending $5,000 a month on Google Ads with no ability to connect that spend to actual booked appointments.

The tools existed to solve each of these problems individually. But nobody had built a platform that solved all of them together — in one system, designed around how med spas actually operate.

What We Believe

Four principles that shape every decision.

Technology should be invisible.

The best software disappears into your workflow. You shouldn’t need a training manual to send an email campaign or a certification to read your dashboard. [PRODUCT_NAME] is built to be intuitive from the first login — because your team is busy caring for patients, not learning software.

AI should take action, not just answer questions.

A chatbot that can explain your Botox pricing is nice. An AI agent that can book the appointment, create the contact record, send the confirmation text, and trigger a follow-up workflow is transformative. We build AI that does real work — not AI that performs.

Your data belongs in one place.

Every disconnected tool creates a blind spot. When your CRM doesn’t talk to your booking system, and your booking system doesn’t talk to your marketing platform, you can’t see the full picture.
