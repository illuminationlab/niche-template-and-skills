How the math works

The assumptions behind your number.

Missed calls. The average med spa ticket sits around $500 across injectables, laser, and facial services. When a prospective patient calls and hits voicemail, roughly 85% never call back — they move on to the next practice in the search results. Multiply your daily missed-call volume by your average ticket and that 85% drop-off across 260 working days, and the number gets large fast.

No-shows. Industry benchmarks put med spa no-show rates between 10% and 25%. Even with deposits and late-cancel fees, roughly half of every no-show slot goes unfilled in time — meaning the chair stayed empty, the provider was paid, and the revenue vanished.

What the calculator does not yet include. Unresponded web leads, lapsed patients past their rebooking window, and review-driven local-search losses. In other words: the number you see here is a floor, not a ceiling.
