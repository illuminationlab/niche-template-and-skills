Getting Started Guide

From Signup to Booked Appointments:
Your First 30 Days with [PRODUCT_NAME].

A practical, week-by-week walkthrough for new customers. No filler — just the exact steps that take you from a blank workspace to a platform that's answering calls, nurturing leads, and filling your calendar.

Days 1–3

Account setup and configuration

Your first 72 hours are about laying a clean foundation. Most of this is pointing [PRODUCT_NAME] at the phone numbers, calendars, and patient records you already have — so the automations you turn on later have clean data to work with.

  • Complete the platform walkthrough with your onboarding specialist
  • Connect or port your main business phone number into Voice AI
  • Set up provider calendars, working hours, and room availability
  • Import existing patient contacts and tag by service history
  • Configure your appointment types and full treatment menu
  • Add your brand colors, logo, and practice details for templates
Days 3–5

Training your AI agents

Voice AI and Conversation AI only sound as good as the knowledge you give them. This week is about feeding them your practice's voice, services, and edge cases so they can handle 80% of inbound interactions without a human.

  • Build your custom knowledge base: services, FAQs, hours, and policies
  • Configure Voice AI greeting, personality, and escalation rules
  • Set up Conversation AI on your website and social channels
  • Load pricing rules, consult requirements, and pre-care instructions
  • Run test conversations and refine responses until they sound like you
  • Approve your AI agents to go live on real inbound traffic
Days 5–10

Launching your core automations

This is where [PRODUCT_NAME] starts earning its keep. The five automations below are the ones that move revenue in week one — and they're all pre-built, so turning them on is a matter of reviewing copy and flipping switches.

  • Enable missed-call text-back (captures voicemail traffic instantly)
  • Activate appointment reminder sequences (SMS + email, timed)
  • Turn on post-treatment review requests via Reputation AI
  • Launch the new-lead nurture sequence across email and SMS
  • Schedule the lapsed-patient re-engagement campaign
  • Confirm every automation has been test-fired before going live
Days 10–15

Building your first campaign

With your foundation and automations running, it's time to start driving new traffic. Pick your highest-demand service, build one beautiful landing page, and pair it with a matched broadcast and ad spend.

  • Create a landing page for your top-performing or newest service
  • Write and schedule an email + SMS broadcast to existing patients
  • Connect Google and Meta ad accounts to [PRODUCT_NAME] tracking
  • Launch a starter ad campaign pointed at your landing page
  • Confirm lead capture, attribution, and automation routing end-to-end
Days 15–30

Measuring results and optimizing

The second half of your first month is data. You now have two weeks of automation history, campaign results, and inbound-call analytics to learn from. This is where [PRODUCT_NAME] stops being a tool and starts being a growth engine.

  • Read your analytics dashboard and identify your top-3 channels
  • Review attribution data to see which ads actually drive bookings
  • Adjust automation timing and copy based on open and reply rates
  • Set your 90-day KPI benchmarks (lead-to-book, no-show, rebooking)
  • Schedule your first optimization review with your success manager
